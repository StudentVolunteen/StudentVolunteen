Our Mission

VolunTEEN is a platform that lets you keep track of your volunteer hours without hassle and also recommends volunteering opportunities for you to do. It keeps everything related to your volunteering including logging hours, signing up for events, and getting your hours approved in one place making it very convenient and easy to use.
