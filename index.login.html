Our Plan

Our researched plan ensures the best experience for both Students and Administrators

Students

Need to keep track of your volunteer hours? Our website makes it a breeze! Easily log your hours and request certification with just a few clicks. Simply enter your supervisor's email, and they'll receive a notification about your completed activity. With a quick "yes" or "no" and their electronic signature, your hours will be officially certified and recorded. Want to find easily find new volunteer opportunities? Look no further! Our website features a wide range of volunteer options you can easily sign up for.

Administrators

Using this website, you will be ensuring the credibility of the hours that your students have completed. Also be able to organize and promote your own volunteer events to attract more participants and make a bigger impact.

About Us

Volunteen is a platform that lets you keep track of you volunteer hours without hassle and also recommends volunteering opportunites for you to do. It keeps everything related to your volunteering including logging hours, signing up for events, and getting your hours approved in one place making it very convenient and easy to use.
